FEEL FREE TO WORK ON YOUR PROJECT

Members are welcome to work on their projects at Xinfab, but we are not a co-working space or a café. This means that you cannot use our space to work on your Chinese homework, for example. We are a fablab and a maker community, so we want to make sure those who come to Xinfab to actually make things have the space to do so.

 

PLAY, CREATE, MAKE, FAB

As a fablab and maker space, Xinfab is the place where members can play, experiment, create, make and fabricate things. However, machines cannot be used for mass production. We are not a factory.

 

EVERYBODY HAS THEIR TURN

Members must book machines in advance. This is to ensure everyone gets their turn and that nobody needs to wait.

 

No show: if you book a machine and don't cancel your booking in advance or don't show up, credits are deducted from your membership anyway. The time you book but don't use is time that somebody else could have used.
